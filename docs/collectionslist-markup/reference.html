Abe Weisburd Scrapbook (TAM 450)

Description
1 box (.5 feet). Abe Weisburd was a progressive activist who wrote regularly for the communist weekly paper The Guardian. This collection is a scrapbook presented to Abe by family and friends on his 70th birthday. It contains writings by Weisburd, biographical notes, photos, and letters from friends and family talking about Weisburd.
